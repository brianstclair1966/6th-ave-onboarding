# How the One Stop Shop Works For You

## What Makes Us Different

6th Ave is built as a one-stop shop. Most brokerages make you source everything separately—an agent for buying, a designer for interiors, a construction company for renovations.

We built the infrastructure so your clients don't have to do that. When your clients want to renovate, you have a partner. When they need design help, it's built in. When they want to refinance, we have resources.

For you, this means:
- More deal velocity (one-stop shopping closes faster)
- Stronger relationships (you're the connector)
- Higher perceived value (you solve multiple problems)
- Better margins (you capture referral ecosystem revenue)
