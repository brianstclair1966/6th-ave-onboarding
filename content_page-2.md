# Who We Are & How We Operate

## Our Foundation

6th Ave Homes exists to help you find and build a home that feels like you. We're not a transaction factory. We're built on the belief that real estate is deeply personal.

**[Read our full Mission, Vision & Core Values](https://www.6thavehomesagents.com/mission-and-values)**

Our core values guide everything:
- We do what's right, even when it's harder
- We build relationships that matter
- We bring expertise and care to every transaction
- We're committed to your success

**[Your 90-Day Roadmap](https://www.dropbox.com/scl/fi/5sll74lw1ydy27cb2hotg/6th-Ave-Homes-Language-Roadmap-05.24.pdf)** — This is your strategic north star. Reference it throughout your first 90 days.

## How We Build Business

We call it "Networking with Soul."

Most brokerages focus on closing transactions. We focus on building relationships. That means:
- Being genuinely interested in people, not just deals
- Showing up consistently in your community
- Creating content that reflects your authentic voice
- Helping others before asking for business

This isn't a tactic. It's how we actually operate.

**[Learn more about Networking with Soul](https://www.6thavehomesagents.com/social-media-with-soul)**
