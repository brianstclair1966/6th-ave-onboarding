# Welcome to the Team

You're about to join something special. This onboarding process is designed to get you active, confident, and ready to build your business in 1–2 weeks.

---

## Your 8-Step Activation Path

Follow these steps in order. Most happen in parallel, which means you can work on multiple steps simultaneously.

### **Step 1: Emergency Contact Form**
We need some basic information for our office files.

[**Fill out this form →**](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSe8qn_VAEFbhRuSt6mBha39ARx3x8sVS49N3MNqCGVcO0SPPQ/viewform)

**Time:** 5 minutes

---

### **Step 2: License Sponsorship**
We'll send a sponsorship request through TREC. **Accept this invite in your email promptly** — you can't proceed without it.

**Time:** Watch for email, accept within 24 hours

---

### **Step 3: Join Your Local MLS**
This is critical. You need MLS access to list, search, and manage transactions.

**DFW Metroplex:** Contact [membership@GFWAR.org](mailto:membership@GFWAR.org)
- Include: you're a newly licensed 6th Ave agent
- Also: order your SUPRA key & download the eKEY app
- Complete: Matrix MLS training & Ethics class (online, ~1 hour)

**Bell County:** [Join TBBOR →](https://mytbbor.com/join) (Office: 908 Bryan Ave, Fort Worth TX 76104)

**Time:** 3–5 days (includes training classes)

---

### **Step 4: Sign Independent Contractor Agreement**
You're an independent contractor, not an employee. We'll send this via DocuSign.

**Key points:** You manage your own hours, keep your own income, handle your own taxes.

**Time:** 10 minutes to review & sign

---

### **Step 5: Write Your Website Bio**
Agents are featured on 6thavehomes.com. [**See an example bio →**](https://6thavehomes.com/brian-st-clair)

Send your finished bio to [brian@6thavehomes.com](mailto:brian@6thavehomes.com)

Also complete: [**"All About You" form →**](https://forms.gle/LRHYkUAuX51Hyv5E6)

We do quarterly headshot shoots — you'll get scheduled then.

**Time:** 30 minutes

---

### **Step 6: Create Your IABS Form**
This is **critical for compliance.** You must give this to every client before showing a property.

[**Download the 6th Ave template →**](https://drive.google.com/file/d/18aEHlMDTcs4OffcBoz9r2YbfS3-1AP7e/view?usp=sharing)

Save your filled PDF to Google Drive, get the share link, and add it to your email signature with a warning about wire fraud.

**Time:** 20 minutes

---

### **Step 7: Get Started on Rechat**
This is your client relationship tool. Start here: [**Rechat Getting Started Guide →**](https://help.rechat.com/guides/getting-started)

Work through the first section, then explore the [**Deals section →**](https://help.rechat.com/guides/deals)

More Rechat training comes in your Guide Training sessions.

**Time:** 30 minutes initial

---

### **Step 8: Review Our Guide Training**
[**Go to Guide Training Sessions →**](https://www.6thavehomesagents.com/guide-training-program)

Read Sessions 1, 2, and 3. These set the foundation for how we work, our philosophy, and your role.

**Time:** 1–2 hours (can do this anytime)

---

## What Happens While You're Doing This

While you're working through steps 1–7, our team will:
- Order your business cards
- Create your Google Suite (Gmail, Drive, Calendar)
- Set you up on Slack, Rechat, Realscout, DocuSign

You'll get setup links by email. Check your inbox regularly.

---

## Timeline

**Days 1–2:** Steps 1, 2, 4
**Days 2–5:** Steps 3 (MLS training)
**Days 3–5:** Steps 5, 6, 7 (in parallel with MLS)
**Anytime:** Step 8 (Guide Training)

You'll be ready to list properties around **Day 5–7**.
