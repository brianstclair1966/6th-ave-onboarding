## Your First Week

**Days 1–2:** Complete intake + start licensing
- Fill out emergency contact form
- Get TREC sponsorship request (accept the email immediately)

**Days 2–5:** Join your MLS
- Contact your local association (GFWAR for DFW, TBBOR for Bell County)
- Complete required training (Matrix MLS, NAR Ethics)

**Days 3–5:** Parallel with MLS
- Sign your Independent Contractor Agreement
- Write your website bio
- Submit your headshot form
