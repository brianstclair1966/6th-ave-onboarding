# Getting You Active

Steps 1–3 are straightforward and happen in parallel. Don't overthink them — just execute.

## Step 2: TREC Sponsorship

**What happens:** We submit a sponsorship request through TREC (Texas Real Estate Commission).

**What you do:** Watch for an email from TREC. Click the accept link **within 24 hours** of receiving it.

**Why it matters:** You can't get MLS access without being TREC-licensed under 6th Ave.

**Timeline:** 24–48 hours

---

## Step 3: Join Your Local MLS

This is the big one. MLS access unlocks everything.

### **If you're in DFW Metroplex (most agents):**

Contact **[membership@GFWAR.org](mailto:membership@GFWAR.org)** and say:
> "I'm a newly licensed real estate agent with 6th Ave Homes. I'd like to apply for membership."

**Also required:**
1. **SUPRA Key** — Order your first SUPRA lockbox key (GFWAR will guide you)
2. **eKEY App** — Download the SUPRA eKEY app to your phone
3. **Matrix MLS Training** — GFWAR will schedule this (video-based, ~2 hours)
4. **NAR Code of Ethics** — Online course, ~1 hour, can do anytime
5. **Create your Matrix account** — Once you have MLS access

**Timeline:** 3–5 days

---

### **If you're in Bell County:**

[**Go to TBBOR and apply →**](https://mytbbor.com/join)

**On the form:**
- Register as "Primary Member"
- Office address: **908 Bryan Ave, Fort Worth, TX 76104**

Once approved, order your SUPRA key and download eKEY.

**Timeline:** 3–5 days

## Common Questions

**Q: What if TREC takes forever?**
It usually doesn't, but if it does, follow up after 48 hours. We can help.

**Q: Can I start looking at listings before MLS is active?**
No. You need MLS access to search and manage transactions. This is non-negotiable.

**Q: What if GFWAR/TBBOR is slow?**
Call them. It's your license, your timeline. Be proactive.
