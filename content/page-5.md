# Step 8: Learn How We Think

This is your orientation to how 6th Ave works. The **Guide Training Sessions** are three deep dives into our philosophy, compliance, and brand.

---

## The Three Guide Training Sessions

[**→ Go to Guide Training Program**](https://www.6thavehomesagents.com/guide-training-program)

### **Session 1: Your Role as a Guide**

What does it mean to work for 6th Ave? This covers:
- Our philosophy (relationship-first real estate)
- Your role (what we expect, what you can expect)
- How we build business (authenticity, trust, long-term thinking)

**Read this first.** It sets the foundation for everything else.

### **Session 2: Compliance & the CDA Process**

Don't let the name intimidate you. This is about:
- How we handle client data (safely, legally, ethically)
- The CDA (Client Data Agreement) process
- What clients need to know about how we work
- Your responsibilities as a licensed agent

**Time:** 45 minutes

### **Session 3: Brand Standards & Marketing**

When you represent 6th Ave, you represent us well. This covers:
- Our brand — how it looks, feels, sounds
- How to market yourself authentically
- Our logos, colors, tone
- Content standards

**Time:** 30 minutes

---

## Timeline for These Sessions

You don't need to finish them before you can work. But **do read them in your first week.**

- **Day 1:** Read Session 1 (understand your role)
- **Day 3:** Read Session 2 (understand compliance)
- **Day 5:** Read Session 3 (understand our brand)

By the end of week one, you'll have the foundational knowledge every 6th Ave agent needs.

---

## What Comes After

Once you're through the 8 steps and the Guide Training:

- Your MLS should be active
- You'll have system access (Rechat, Slack, Realscout, etc.)
- Your website bio will be live
- You're ready to **list your first property**

That's when the real work begins. And we'll be here to support you.
